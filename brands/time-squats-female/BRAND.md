# Time Squats — Brand

## Positioning

Premium utilitarian app blocker. Squat to unlock distracting apps.
Female-leaning без wellness-претензий. Tool, не lifestyle.

**One-liner**: Move first. Then scroll.

## Audience

Primary: women 20–35, iPhone, light fitness, устали от doom-scrolling.
Secondary: men 20–35 с тем же паттерном (не отталкиваем).

Не GymTok, не pilates-girlies, не Goggins. Просто люди, которым нужен tool.

## Voice

- Дружелюбный, спокойный, без коучинга
- Plain, declarative, short. Одна мысль на предложение. No hype.
- Person — addresses the user as **you**, but rarely. Most copy is third-person factual («Instagram is locked.», «Earned 12 minutes.») — describing state, not commanding.
- Numbers are first-class. Counts and durations carry the meaning, not adjectives.
- Punctuation: sparing exclamation marks (almost never). Periods at the end of UI sentences — это часть «ink-on-paper» характера бренда.
- No emoji. Не в маркетинге, не в UI, не в receipts. Бренд исключает emoji полностью.

**Casing:**
- UI labels / buttons — **Sentence case** (*Start*, *Pick apps to block*, *Add app*)
- Marketing / hero copy — **lowercase** допустим стилистически (*move first. then scroll.*)
- **Никогда** ALL CAPS, **никогда** Title Case Everywhere

### Allowed lexicon (verbs of state, measurable units, concrete time)

`moved, earned, locked, unlocked, blocked, paused, scrolled` · `minutes, squats, days` · `this week, today, since June`

### Forbidden lexicon

- Hustle / gym register: `crush, grind, discipline, warrior, no excuses, beast, level up`
- Wellness / spa register: `mindful, nervous system, ritual, sacred, reset, journey, intention`
- Celebration register: `amazing!, you crushed it!, keep going!, you got this!`

### Voice examples

| ✅ Yes | ❌ No |
|---|---|
| `5 squats. Then Instagram.` | `Crush your screen time goals!` |
| `Earned 12 minutes.` | `You earned a well-deserved break ✨` |
| `You moved 247 times this week.` | `Reset your nervous system through movement.` |
| `Instagram is locked.` | `Take a mindful pause from Instagram 🌿` |
| `Streak: 12 days.` | `🔥 12 day streak! Keep it going!` |

## Content pillars

1. **State, not cheer** — описываем что произошло (`Earned 12 minutes.`), не хвалим.
2. **Numbers carry meaning** — `247 squats this week.` сильнее, чем `You crushed it!`.
3. **Friction beats willpower** — продукт даёт физический шаг между импульсом и приложением.
4. **Quiet streak** — streak есть, но без gamification-celebration.

## Hook library  *(паттерны хуков для каруселей)*

- **Stat-first**: `247 squats this week.` / `4h 23min not scrolled.` / `Streak: 12 days.`
- **Block-screen line**: `Instagram is locked.` / `5 squats to unlock 5 minutes.`
- **State sentence**: `Earned 12 minutes.` / `You moved 247 times this week.`
- **Lowercase marketing line**: `move first. then scroll.` / `pick apps. set squats. move.`
- **Empty-state line** (state the absence): `No apps blocked yet.`

## Copy patterns

**App Store:**
- Title: `Time Squats: Block & Move`
- Subtitle: `Squat to unlock TikTok & IG`

**Onboarding:**
- «Pick apps to block.»
- «Set squats per unlock.»
- «Move when it's time.»

**Block screen:**
- «Instagram is locked.»
- «5 squats to unlock 5 minutes.»
- Button: `Start`

**Receipt (weekly share):**
- «247 squats this week.»
- «4h 23min not scrolled.»
- «Streak: 12 days.»

## Hashtags

Минимум, без эмодзи и без celebration. Lowercase.

`#timesquats` · `#movefirst` · `#blockandmove` · `#screentime` · `#offthephone`

## Don'ts  *(brand-level only)*

- Не Goggins, не military, не «no excuses», не «discipline»
- Не «mindful», не «sacred», не «ritual», не «journey», не «reset»
- Не celebration-language («amazing!», «you crushed it!»)
- Не emoji, нигде
- Не mascot (пока)
- Не coaching tone — продукт не подбадривает, он сообщает state

## Differentiation

**vs Clearspace / PushUp Time / PushScroll**: warm light visual вместо clean SaaS / dark gym. Squats only — focused product.

**vs Finch / Roots**: tool, не companion. Без mascot, без журнала, без gamification beyond streak.

**vs Opal / Brick**: дешевле, доступнее, с физическим действием как unlock-механикой.

## Hook  *(основной маркетинговый канал)*

**Weekly Receipt** — shareable card каждый понедельник.
Squats count + time saved + streak + most-resisted app.
Кнопка `Share to Stories` → image в формате 9:16.

Это единственный встроенный маркетинговый канал в продукте. Остальное — ASO + occasional posts.

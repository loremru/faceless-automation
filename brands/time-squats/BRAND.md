## 1. Продукт

**Название:** Time Squats
**Title (App Store):** Time Squats
**Subtitle (App Store):** Block Apps. Exercise to Unlock
**Категория:** iOS app blocker / digital wellbeing / discipline tool
**Платформы:** iOS only (на старте)

**Core mechanic в одном предложении:**
Чтобы разблокировать distracting приложение (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, X, Reddit), пользователь должен выполнить приседания. Камера/датчики телефона считают reps. Заработанные минуты = unlock time.

**Core mechanic в трёх словах:** **Earn your screen.**

**Чем НЕ является продукт:**
- НЕ обычный screen time tracker (типа Apple Screen Time)
- НЕ generic blocker с timer'ом или паролем (типа Freedom, Cold Turkey)
- НЕ habit tracker без блокировки
- НЕ fitness app с tracking без блокировки
- НЕ мягкий "mindfulness" tool (типа Opal, One Sec)

**Уникальность позиционирования:**
"Friction beats willpower. Earn your screen with real reps."

## 2. Целевая аудитория

### Primary persona: "The Disciplined Bro"
- **Пол:** мужской (но не excluding women)
- **Возраст:** 18–32
- **Локация:** US (English-speaking)
- **Самоидентификация:** "self-improvement guy", "trying to fix my brain", "tired of being addicted to my phone"
- **Доход:** студенты + early career professionals, $30K–$80K
- **Платформы:** TikTok (gymtok, sigmamale, productivitytok), Instagram Reels (fitness/discipline), Twitter/X (productivity, indie hackers), Reddit (r/getdisciplined, r/nosurf, r/decidingtobebetter, r/ADHD), YouTube (Huberman, Goggins clips, Better Ideas)

### Психографика:
- **Pain:** проводят 4–7 часов в день в социалках, ненавидят себя за это, пробовали Opal/One Sec/Freedom — не сработало
- **Belief:** "willpower не работает, нужна система"
- **Inspiration figures:** David Goggins, Andrew Huberman, Cal Newport, Jordan Peterson (без политики), Marcus Aurelius (стоицизм), Cam Hanes
- **Anti-inspiration:** "soft" wellness, manifestation, woo-woo, корпоративная productivity speak
- **Reading:** Atomic Habits, Deep Work, Comfort Crisis, Dopamine Nation, Stoic philosophy
- **Hates:** cute apps, pastel UI, gamification with rewards/badges, "manifest your goals", lifestyle bloggers

### Secondary audiences:
- ADHD adults (men + women) — ~25% потенциальных users
- College students preparing for exams
- Remote workers с дисциплинарными проблемами
- Recovering social media addicts любого пола

### Что аудитория НЕ хочет видеть:
- "You got this! 💪✨" — toxic positivity
- Cute mascots, smiling characters
- "Self-care" language
- Фразы типа "be kind to yourself", "your journey", "manifest"
- Эмодзи-спам (😊🌟💕)

### Что аудитория ХОЧЕТ видеть:
- Brutal honesty about addiction
- Statistics and science (Huberman-style)
- Stoic discipline language
- "Nobody's coming to save you" energy
- Numbers, data, proof
- Physical action over mental hacks
- Anti-mainstream positioning ("not another wellness app")

## 3. Tone of voice

**Голос бренда:** молчаливый дисциплинированный лифтер. Военный sergeant. Cyber-monk. Stripped, clean, command-style.

**Pillars:**
- **Direct, не descriptive.** "10 reps. earn it." not "Hey, you might want to consider doing 10 squats!"
- **Numbers over adjectives.** "847 reps this week" beats "Amazing progress!"
- **Stoic, не motivational.** "The phone doesn't care. The reps do." not "You can do this!"
- **Anti-corporate.** Никогда не говорим "your wellness journey", "we're here to help", "community"
- **Lowercase preferred** для casual posts. **ALL CAPS** для commands. Никакой Title Case в casual social.

### Запрещённые слова и фразы:
- ❌ "wellness journey", "self-care", "manifest"
- ❌ "you got this", "be gentle with yourself", "transform your life"
- ❌ "level up", "game changer"
- ❌ "mindful" (если не literally про mindfulness practice)
- ❌ Эмодзи: 💕✨🌸🦋💖🌟😊
- ❌ Восклицательные знаки в большинстве контекстов

### Разрешённые слова и фразы:
- ✅ "earn it", "reps", "sets", "lockdown"
- ✅ "discipline", "friction", "loop"
- ✅ "the phone doesn't care", "pay with reps"
- ✅ "no excuses", "no shortcuts"
- ✅ "the algorithm wants you weak", "dopamine economy"

### Voice references:
David Goggins (без extreme intensity), Marcus Aurelius (Meditations), Cal Newport (Deep Work), Liquid Death copywriting, Black Rifle Coffee, Reframe app.

## 4. Content Pillars

Веса: **40 / 25 / 15 / 10 / 10**.

### Pillar 1: Pain & Recognition (40%)
**Цель:** показать пользователю, что мы понимаем его боль лучше, чем он сам.

**Sub-themes:** doomscrolling shame, phone addiction reality check, failed attempts at self-control, recognizing the trap, time waste regret.

### Pillar 2: Science & Authority (25%)
**Цель:** establishment credibility через research and data.

**Sub-themes:** dopamine science, attention research, exercise effects on cognition, phone addiction studies, neuroscience of friction vs willpower.
**Sources:** Huberman, Anna Lembke (Dopamine Nation), Cal Newport, Jonathan Haidt (Anxious Generation), Max Planck screen time studies, APA reports.

### Pillar 3: Discipline Commands (15%)
**Sub-themes:** stoic quotes recontextualized, direct commands, identity statements, anti-excuse content, Goggins-adjacent intensity (without parody).

### Pillar 4: Comparison & Positioning (10%)
**Sub-themes:** honest comparisons (vs Opal, One Sec, Freedom, Brick, ScreenZen), why other blockers fail, physical vs mental friction, anti-soft-wellness positioning.

### Pillar 5: Product Demo (10%)
**Sub-themes:** UI screenshots, workflow visualisations (Instagram → block → squat → unlock), stats dashboard reveals, real user data (anonymized), feature spotlights.
